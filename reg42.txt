Dear International Student Community,
 
The ISSS is hosting a free workshop for international students on employment-based immigration options in the U.S. for F-1/J-1 students.   The presentation will be led by Attorney William A. Stock, a partner in the law firm of Klasko, Rulon, Stock & Seltzer, and will cover such topics as permanent residency, self-petitions, H1B visa, and other options.  Come and explore what immigration options may be available to you beyond F-1/J-1 status upon completion of your degree.  Specific topics to be covered include:
 
?             Introduction to Permanent Residency
o             Permanent Residency Quotas
o             Employment Based Permanent Residency
o             Self-Petitions
o             Employer Sponsored permanent residence
?             Non-Immigrant Options, Immigrant Intent, and Timing
?             H-1B
?             Other Non-Immigrant options
?             Q & A Session
