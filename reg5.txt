Postdoctoral Research Position at the University of Windsor.

The Department of Mathematics and Statistics at the University of Windsor invites applications for
a postdoctoral fellow to work under the direction of Professor Abdo Alfakih. The position is subject to
budgetary approval and the salary is competitive.

The Applicant should have a Ph.D. degree in mathematics, computer science, operations research or
any other closely related field. Research experience in one of the following areas is
highly desirable: semi-definite programming, combinatorial
optimization, rigidity theory.  The appointment is for one year and the
starting date is flexible with preference given to those who can start as soon as possible.

Applicants should submit a cover letter, CV and should arrange for three letters of recommendation
to be sent by email to alfakih@uwindsor.ca.

Applications will be accepted until the position is filled.
